# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to foster an open and welcoming environment. We are open to participation by everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity.

## Our Standards

### Positive behaviors

- 🫂 Using respectful and inclusive language
- 🧠 Giving constructive feedback
- 🤝 Being open to different perspectives
- 🐛 Accepting and correcting mistakes
- 🌟 Prioritizing what's best for the community

### Unacceptable behaviors

- ❌ Sexist/racist/abusive language
- ❌ Personal attacks
- ❌ Harassment (public/private)
- ❌ Sharing private information without consent
- ❌ Unprofessional conduct

## Enforcement

Report violations to @Pasa_38 via Telegram or GitHub. All complaints will be reviewed and appropriate action will be taken.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces (GitHub Issues, PR, Discord, Telegram).
